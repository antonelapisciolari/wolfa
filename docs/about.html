About Us

We are two experienced professionals in the field of Quality Assurance (QA) with a clear vision: to share their knowledge and skills to help companies improve their software development processes and, at the same time, promote the personal and professional growth of other professionals in the field.

After working together in various companies and projects, we realized that our combination of technical skills and ability to work as a team was something special. Together, we complemented each other and covered all the necessary aspects to carry out an entrepreneurship in the Testing field.

This is how we decided to start and found our own Testing company, which not only offered QA services to companies but also offered courses and training for other professionals who wanted to improve in their careers.
